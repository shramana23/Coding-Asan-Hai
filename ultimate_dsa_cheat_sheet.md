## 💡 How to use this sheet

1. **Study by topic**, not by difficulty — pick one topic per week
2. **Identify the pattern** before looking at the solution (Sliding Window? Two Pointers? DFS?)
3. **Solve → Review → Revisit** after 3 days (spaced repetition)
4. Aim for **3–5 problems/day** during active prep
